Welcome to my website.

I created this site as a home for my research and as a place to collect notes over time. My interests lie at the intersection of AI, learning, and decision-making, especially in problems where mathematically grounded methods meet practical systems.

I am particularly drawn to questions that connect research with the design of intelligent systems, and more broadly to the ways AI may shape engineering, products, and decision-making in the real world.

This site will include papers, talks, project updates, and occasional notes as my work develops. I hope it becomes a useful record of what I am building and thinking about.

Thanks for visiting.
